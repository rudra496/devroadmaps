FAQ

Is DevRoadmaps really free? ▼
Yes! 100% free and open source under the MIT license. All resources linked are free too. No premium tiers, no sign-ups, no paywalls.
How is this different from roadmap.sh? ▼
roadmap.sh is a great project but has moved to a paid model for interactive features. DevRoadmaps is fully open source, interactive by default, and community-driven. Every feature is free forever.
How does progress tracking work? ▼
Your progress is stored in your browser's localStorage. No server, no account — it just works. Note: clearing your browser data will reset progress.
Can I contribute a roadmap? ▼
Absolutely! Check out CONTRIBUTING.md for instructions. You can add new roadmaps, suggest resources, or improve existing ones via pull request.
Are the resources vetted? ▼
Yes — all resources are hand-picked from trusted sources like MDN, freeCodeCamp, official docs, and quality YouTube channels. Every URL is verified to be free and accessible.
